ANY WAR

Close-Combat-style WW2 tactics. Here's how to play.

THE MISSION

You command the US assault (blue). Take the crossroads objective and hold it for 60 seconds before the clock runs out. The Germans (red) defend it.

GETTING STARTED

The battle is already underway — your forces start drawn up in position. Take command at once: pick a unit and give it orders. Press O any time to look at the objective.

SELECT YOUR MEN

  • Click a soldier, or its card in the UNITS panel on the left.
  • Drag a box across several squads to grab them all at once.
  • Shift-click a squad (on the map or its card) to add or remove it from the group.
  • Ctrl/⌘ + 1‑9 stores the selected group; press 1‑9 to recall it. A group moves as one, keeping its formation.

GIVE ORDERS

With a unit selected, pick an order, then click the map:

QMove — advance at a steady pace
WFast — rush (but easier to spot)
ESneak — slow, stay hidden
RDefend — hold & face the threat
TAmbush — hold fire for a deadly first volley
FFire — click an enemy to focus, or ground to suppress
GSmoke — mortar teams: screen an advance
CCharge — cavalry: gallop in and ride the enemy down
SpacePause / resume

Right-drag to pan · scroll to zoom · arrow keys also pan.

YOUR UNITS

  • RIFLE — line infantry; the backbone of the assault.
  • MG — light machine gun; superb at suppressing the enemy so others can move.
  • AT — bazooka team; kills tanks — aim for the flank or rear.
  • MORTAR — one tube, slow to reload; lobs HE or smoke over walls. You must call the shot.
  • TANK — your Sherman; click to direct its fire.

TACTICS

  • Most fire suppresses rather than kills — pin the enemy, then flank.
  • Buildings, hedges and woods give cover and concealment; smoke blocks line of sight.
  • Watch morale: pinned or routing men won't fight. Lead with grenades and MGs.

MULTIPLAYER

Just share the page link. The first person to open it hosts as the US; the next takes command of the Germans; anyone after that spectates. The badge in the bottom-right shows your role.
